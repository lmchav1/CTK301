Hello! I am Luz Chavez. I am currently 20 years old and attending Illinois State University. I am majoring in Game Design and am expected to graduate in 2026. I spend a lot of my time watching movies or shows but if I am not bingewatching I am doing art! My main forms of media are digital or traditional. I specialize in original character artwork or work for social media. My hope in the Game Design field is to work on character design or the storytelling aspect of games.

About my gallery: Here I have pulled works from my Instagram (@kyuu.draws) and some pieces I had done digitally on my Ipad on Procreate. Most of these are just character drawings, of no one in particular, mainly just characters I have just randomly drawn and come up with. There is no plan when I go into drawing, I begin by figuring out a pose first and then add pieces that I think fit and belong and once I see a clearer image I refine it to its final piece. I always try to make sure my pieces have some sort of story or at least emotion. In the end, my art is more for my own pleasure, but I love to share it with others if possible.
